Article content
• Earnings were US$7.46 a share in the period ended June 29. Analysts had predicted US$7.30 on average, according to data compiled by Bloomberg.

• Revenue rose to US$35.3-billion, compared with analyst estimates of US$35-billion.

• Sales of iPhones in the third-quarter totalled 31.2 million, ahead of analyst expectations, while iPads came in below expectations with 14.6 million units sold. Analysts had estimated 26.1 million iPhones and 17.3 iPads sold

• Apple shares rose more than 5% to US$441 in extended trading after the results were released. They closed at US$418.99 earlier in New York.
